Frequently Asked Questions — Screws

What is the difference between self-tapping and self-drilling screws?

Self-tapping screws form their own thread in a pre-drilled hole, while self-drilling screws have a drill-bit tip that bores and threads in one step without pre-drilling.

What is the minimum order quantity for screws?

Do you supply high-tensile socket cap screws?
