How to Order — Our Export Process

Simple, transparent, and proven. Here is exactly what happens from your first message to the moment your shipment arrives.

End-to-End Clarity

No Surprises. No Guesswork.

Many first-time importers hesitate because the process feels unfamiliar. We remove that barrier completely. Below is every step we take together — from your inquiry to your delivery — so you always know what to expect and what comes next.

1

Inquiry Day 1

Send us your requirement via our enquiry form, email (sales@ternsexim.com), or WhatsApp. Include product, grade, quantity, and destination.

2

Quotation Within 24 hrs

We respond within 24 working hours with a detailed quotation: unit price, Incoterms (FOB/CIF), lead time, and payment terms.

3

Proforma Invoice

On confirmation, we issue a Proforma Invoice (PI) detailing specifications, quantity, pricing, and terms for your approval.

4

Payment Terms

Trial and first orders: 100% advance. Established buyers (after 5–8 successful shipments): 70–80% advance, balance against shipment. Payment via Wire Transfer (T/T) or Letter of Credit (L/C).

5

Manufacturing 7–21 working days

Production begins on payment confirmation. Manufacturing and dispatch: 7–21 working days, depending on product, quantity, and specifications.

6

Quality Inspection

Every order undergoes quality checks and is supplied with Mill Test Certificates (MTC). Third-party inspection (SGS / BV / TÜV) available on request.

7

Shipping

Primarily sea freight; air freight available for urgent or sample shipments. Shipped on FOB or CIF terms from any major Indian port (Chennai, Tuticorin, Mumbai/Nhava Sheva, Mundra).

8

Balance Payment

For established-buyer orders, the balance is settled against a copy of the Bill of Lading (B/L) before documents are released.

9

Export Documents

We provide the complete export document set: Bill of Lading, Commercial Invoice, Mill Test Certificate, and Packing List.

10

Delivery Destination port

Your shipment arrives at the destination port. We remain available for support throughout customs clearance and beyond.

From first inquiry to final delivery, our team guides you at every step.
