Buyer FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions — Hex Bolts

What is the difference between DIN 931 and DIN 933 hex bolts?

DIN 931 hex bolts are partially threaded (shank under the head is unthreaded), while DIN 933 hex bolts are fully threaded from head to tip. Choose DIN 931 for shear loads and DIN 933 for full-grip clamping.

What is the minimum order quantity for hex bolts?

Our MOQ is 10,000 pieces or 700 kg depending on bolt size.

Do you supply hot-dip galvanized hex bolts for export?

Yes, we supply hex bolts in plain, zinc-plated, and hot-dip galvanized finishes for corrosion resistance in marine and outdoor applications.
